# 📚 Study OS

> A premium productivity and study management system designed for students and competitive exam aspirants who want to take their learning to the next level.

## 🎯 What is Study OS?

Study OS is more than just another study app—it's your personal academic operating system. Whether you're preparing for competitive exams, managing coursework, or building better learning habits, Study OS gives you the tools to stay organized, focused, and motivated on your journey to success.

Think of it as having a personal productivity coach, study planner, and performance analyst all rolled into one beautiful, intuitive platform.

## ✨ Key Features

### 📋 **Smart Task Planning**
- Break down your study goals into manageable tasks

- Set priorities and track progress in real-time

### 🎯 **Focus Tracking**
- Monitor your study sessions with precision

- Write and organize notes directly within the platform

### 🏆 **Performance Insights**
- Get actionable insights about your study habits
- Personalized recommendations to improve learning efficiency
- Compare performance across different subjects
- Celebrate your wins with achievement tracking

### 🎨 **Beautiful, Intuitive Interface**
- Clean, modern design that's a joy to use
- Works seamlessly across all your devices
- Dark mode for late-night study sessions
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- No installation required—just open and start studying!

### Quick Start

1. **Clone the repository** (if self-hosting)
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/snehavish595/study-os.git
   cd study-os
   ```

2. **Open in your browser**
   - If you're running locally, open `index.html` in your browser
   - Or access the live version at [https://snehavish595.github.io/study-os/](https://snehavish595.github.io/study-os/)

3. **Create your first task**
   - Start by adding subjects you're studying
   - Break down your goals into specific tasks
   - Set deadlines and priority levels

4. **Begin tracking**
   - Use the focus timer to track your study sessions
   - Watch your analytics update in real-time
   - Review your performance insights

## 📖 How to Use Study OS

### 📋 **Task Planning**
1. Click "Add Task" to create a new study task
2. Enter task details: title, subject, deadline, and priority
3. Organize tasks by categories or subjects
4. Mark tasks as complete as you finish them
5. Review completed tasks to track your progress

### ⏱️ **Focus Tracking**
1. Start a focus session from the focus timer
2. Choose your session duration (default: 25 minutes Pomodoro style)
3. Get notified when your session is complete
4. Take breaks between sessions to maintain productivity
5. Watch your focus hours accumulate by subject

### 📊 **Analyzing Your Performance**
1. Visit the Analytics dashboard
2. View charts showing your study distribution
3. Identify your strongest and weakest subjects
4. Check your daily/weekly/monthly progress
5. Use insights to adjust your study plan

### 📝 **Managing Notes**
1. Create notes directly within tasks
2. Use markdown formatting for better organization
3. Tag notes by subject for easy retrieval
4. Search through your notes quickly
5. Link related notes together

## ⚙️ Settings & Customization

- **Theme:** Switch between light and dark modes
- **Focus Timer:** Customize session durations (25 min Pomodoro or custom)
- **Notifications:** Set reminders for upcoming deadlines
- **Data Export:** Download your progress reports
- **Privacy:** All your data stays with you (stored locally)
